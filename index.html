ONAM-2023

Onam is an annual Indian harvest festival celebrated predominantly by the Hindus of Kerala. A major annual event for Keralites, it is the official festival of the state and includes a spectrum of cultural events. Onam commemorates King Mahabali and Vamana.Onam is a harvest festival celebrated in Kerala. It also marks the return of King Mahabali/Maveli in Kerala, as the demon king once ruled the state. According to mythology, the kind-hearted King Mahabali defeated the Gods and began ruling over all three worlds.Lord Vishnu got pleased by Mahabali and ordered him to rule till the end of Kali Yuga, Lord Vishnu blessed Mahabali with a boon that he would be able to visit his kingdom once in a year. So every year people of his kingdom (Kerala), celebrate this Onam festival as the day of home coming of their loving king Mahabali.The legend is of King Asura (popularly known as Maveli), a mythical king who ruled over Kerala.
